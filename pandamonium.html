About This Game

The Back Rooms have haunted a generation. No one knows how they get there; no one knows how to get out. Evil could lurk beyond any corner in this labyrinth. It's your worst nightmare come true, you hear them scratching at the walls, the footsteps echoing, but getting closer. There are signs on the walls, prayers for help. But others are written in human blood. It's the Pandas and they're taunting you.

Can you make your way through the Back Rooms with your Mind and Body intact? Or will the Pandas drag you deeper?
